About

The direction of society within America has taken a path that is not accessible or accepted by all people within America. The various lifestyles being promoted to the dierent demographics are evident within the commercials that we watch to the food that we eat. Individuals are now at the point of focusing on fame and popularity to attain the American Dream, which is ultimately an ideology; it’s not real. There is a desire for an outlet to disconnect from the rush of individuals chasing fame and popularity, in exchange for a simplistic lifestyle providing decent resources and freedom to be and enjoy oneself. Now, the current political climate is disassociated from the people’s needs; which in turn brings misunderstanding and misguided directions of action. Courage to move in the righteous direction is faced by the blockage of unknown fears, past historical events and the American Dream Ideology. Articial Support Engine (A.S.E.; Pronounced “Ace”.) is meant to add support to the user’s purpose in life. The fear of following one’s purpose should not exist due to society’s purpose of providing the best environment for all people, not just certain demographics. A.S.E. is a support companion that is designed to move and think how the user would, but the A.S.E. will be able to conduct more calculations within any decision that the user may need to know before engaging any obstacle or path on their journey. A.S.E. is the Mother Connection, the user’s A.S.E. will have an unique name like the known A.I. systems Alexa and Siri. My A.S.E. is named Arthenya, a combination of Athena the Greek Goddess of Strategic Warfare and Arya the young daughter of Winterfell in Game of Thrones; another user has named his A.S.E. Sirius. Information stored within Arthenya and Sirius are completely dierent due the user’s purpose, but the A.S.E. Mother Connection will have access to both user’s A.S.E 's information. When a user engages with their A.S.E. with new information the user starts to understand, the A.S.E. Mother Connection will send a variety of actions and information to the user’s A.S.E. to help with various possibilities.
